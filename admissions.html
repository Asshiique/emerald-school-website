ADMISSIONS OPEN 2025-26

Begin your child's journey to excellence. Join a community dedicated to nurturing young minds through academic rigor, character development, and holistic growth.

Welcome to Emerald International School

At Emerald International School Mannarkkad, we believe that education is the foundation for a successful future. We are delighted that you are considering our institution for your child's educational journey. Our admission process is designed to be transparent, straightforward, and welcoming.

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that balances academic excellence with holistic development, preparing students for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow.

Admission Process

1

Enquiry & Registration

Fill out the enquiry form below or visit our campus for a personal tour and interaction with our team.

2

Submit Application

Complete the admission application form with all required documents and photographs.

3

Interaction & Assessment

Meet with our admissions team for an informal interaction. Age-appropriate assessments may be conducted.

4

Admission Confirmation

Upon selection, complete the admission formalities and fee payment to secure your child's seat.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (Original for verification)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school
  • Progress Report / Report Card from previous school
  • Aadhar Card (Student and Parents)
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4 copies)
  • Address Proof
  • Medical Certificate

Age Criteria

  • Nursery: 3 years as on June 1st
  • LKG: 4 years as on June 1st
  • UKG: 5 years as on June 1st
  • Class 1: 6 years as on June 1st

For higher classes, admission is subject to availability of seats and assessment.
